ABOUT BLOG

Brief Introduction, September 30,2016

Lizabeth fashion place was founded in 2021 by a young Nigerian lady. Liz was eager to have a career in fashion, which is why she turned to starting her own business and becoming her boss. Being a proud Nigerian, Liz always wanted to incorporate her heritage into the brand, hence the name Lizabeth, which roughly translates to abundance.

Lizabeth fashion place is a global online women's boutique offering sustainable clothing and accessories, both new and vintage. Liz aims to redefine present-day femininity by offerings an array of edgy, fierce, and one-of-a-kind merchandise that reveals the power and sensuality in all of us.
